So stay tuned on the journey of Bhartiya Airways

WHAT WE DO

Meet The Founders

Founder

Mr. Bhagvanta Singh

Mr. Bhagvanta Singh is the founder and Managing Director of Bhartiya Airways. He is currently doing BA+LLB to learn more about the legal side of the business and its development. He launched Bhartiya Airways in 2014 with the name TARA enterprises. By 2019, which became TARA Hotels and Airport Pvt Ltd. To manage airports and travel bookings, He launched Bhartiya Airways. With his years of experience in the management field, he successfully managed other ventures like hotel management, Airport management, and flight booking & holidays packages

CEO
